The active bleaching agent in our whitening gel is Carbamide Peroxide.

This ingredient temporarily enlarges the pores on the enamel allowing the bleaching agent to seep to the layer of dentin in the tooth.

When the oxygen molecules from the bleaching agent come into contact with the discolored stained molecules on the tooth it causes them to break away exposing the natural color of the tooth.
